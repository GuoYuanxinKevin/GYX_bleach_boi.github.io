  • ERG2080: Independent Study: Foundations of Data Science
    Presentation slides  Manuscript
    Supervisor: Prof. Zhiquan Luo
    Record Time: 2019-20, Term 1
    This independent study course covers multiple mathematical subjects that are highly relavant to data science. The reference book is Foundations of Data Science, where Chapter 2,3,4,5,6,8 are mainly discussed. The slides and the manuscript are the materials I used when lecturing on Markov chains and random walks. Some MCMC methods, e.g. Metropolis-Hastings and Gibbs sampler, are introduced. The mixing time of a random walk is also analyzed.
